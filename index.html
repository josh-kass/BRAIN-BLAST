Welcome to Trivia Central, the ultimate trivia game! Here, you can test your knowledge on a wide range of topics, from history and science to pop culture and current events. With over many questions to choose from, you'll never run out of challenges.

To get started, simply choose a difficulty level. Then, answer as many questions as you can in a row without getting a wrong answer. The more questions you answer correctly, the higher your score will be.
